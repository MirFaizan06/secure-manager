                        GETTING STARTED
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. FIRST TIME USE
   - Launch VaultKeeperFree.exe
   - The app creates encrypted storage files automatically
   - Start adding your passwords and documents

2. ADDING PASSWORDS
   - Enter app/website name
   - Enter your password
   - Optionally add category
   - Click "Add Password"

3. ADDING DOCUMENTS
   - Enter document title
   - Enter category
   - Click "Choose File" to select document
   - Click "Add Document"

4. VIEWING PASSWORDS
   - All passwords are hidden by default (‚óè‚óè‚óè‚óè‚óè‚óè‚óè‚óè)
   - Click eye icon (üëÅ) to reveal password
   - Click copy icon (üìã) to copy to clipboard

5. SEARCHING
   - Use search box to filter passwords
   - Search by name or category

‚ö† DATA STORAGE
